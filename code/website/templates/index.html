About the Project

This project is called Flight Experience Feedback Analysis with Sentiment Analysis. It was done by four CSC 400 students — Peter Chinedu Chukwu, Quinton C Haughton, Mohamed Ramzeen, and Joseph Rodriguez — with help from Professor MD Hossain. The goal is to understand what passengers feel about their flight experience by reading and analyzing their reviews.

Methodology

Our Capstone project, Flight Experience Feedback Analysis, focuses on using data science techniques to extract insights from customer flight reviews. We analyze sentiment, categorize feedback, and visualize findings to help improve airline services and enhance customer satisfaction.
